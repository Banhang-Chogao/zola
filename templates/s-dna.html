Sembcorp Design DNA

Data-first. Decoration-last. Beauty from hierarchy.

S-DNA là tài liệu tham khảo thiết kế lấy cảm hứng từ hệ sinh thái Annual Report và Investor Relations của Sembcorp. Đây là documentation only — lựa chọn thiết kế thứ hai bên cạnh Branding Guideline. Không phải redesign, không phải component library, không áp dụng toàn cục.

Nguồn: Interactive Annual Report 2025 · 1H2025 Result Slides

Core anchor: Think Sembcorp Annual Report × Apple Annual Report × Bloomberg × Stripe Docs × McKinsey, not AdminLTE, Crypto Dashboard, glassmorphism, or neon UI.
One sentence: Beauty comes from hierarchy and whitespace, not colors and effects.
{# ===== 01. DESIGN PHILOSOPHY ===== #}
01

Design Philosophy

Enterprise calm. Data-first. Minimal decoration. High information density với whitespace rộng. Slides cảm giác như investor relations, board meeting, annual report — không phải startup pitch deck hay marketing brochure.

Enterprise calm

Trung tính, có cấu trúc, ít nhiễu. Truth > vanity metrics. Decoration stays quiet.

Data-first

Số liệu và insight dẫn dắt layout. Charts second. Animation last.

Cards first

KPI cards là primitive mặc định cho dashboard, stats, investor pages. Tables hỗ trợ, không thay thế.

Strong hierarchy

Large numbers dominate. Labels support. Units whisper. Typography tạo thứ bậc — không phải màu sắc hay hiệu ứng.

Whitespace breathes

Phân vân thì thêm spacing. White space > shadows. Borders > gradients.

Consistency > novelty

Guidelines first. Components later. Evolution, not proliferation. Preserve consistency above novelty.

General blog UI: Apple Annual Report × Bloomberg × Stripe Docs × Notion.
Data-heavy UI (dashboards, KPI, annual reports, investor slides): Sembcorp Annual Report × Apple Annual Report × Bloomberg Terminal × McKinsey Deck.

Soft pastel backgrounds

Light teal, soft blue, soft purple — không saturated, không gradient.

Colored left accent

4px border-left theo segment. Nhận diện nhanh mà không cần icon lớn.

Wide cards

Subtle outline icons

Icon trong circle container, stroke mảnh. Engineering · institutional feeling.

{# ===== 05. ICONS ===== #}
05

Icons

Outline icons · thin strokes · circle containers. Energy, water, city, wind, solar, battery. Institutional feeling — không emoji, không filled, không cartoon, không 3D.

⚡ ○ ▣ ◇ ☀ □

Use

Outline · thin stroke · circle container · monochrome hoặc accent teal.

Avoid

Filled icons · emoji · cartoon · 3D · gradient-filled glyphs.

Recommended libraries

Lucide · Tabler · Phosphor — outline style only.

{# ===== 06. CHARTS DNA ===== #}
06

Charts DNA

Waterfall charts ưu tiên. Stacked bars, horizontal bars, trend lines, donut, map overlays, distribution charts. Minimal legends. No 3D. No rainbow. No decorative charts.

Preferred

Waterfall · stacked bar · horizontal bar · trend line · donut · map overlay · distribution.

Avoid

Pie chart overload · 3D charts · heavy gradients · rainbow palettes · decorative charts.

Chart colors

Positive: teal · Neutral: gray · Secondary: soft blue · Warning: orange · Negative: soft red. Avoid excessive saturation.

Library recommendation

ECharts (waterfall native) · Chart.js · Highcharts. Waterfall charts preferred.

{# ===== 07. SLIDE DNA ===== #}
07

Slide DNA

How to build annual report slides. Investor relations · board meeting · institutional presentation. Not startup pitch · not YouTube thumbnail · not marketing brochure.

Cover

Large title. Minimal text. Strong whitespace. Teal accent line hoặc hero metric nhỏ.

Agenda

2-column layout. Simple numbering (01, 02…). No bullet overload.

KPI page

Hero metrics row. Large values. Tiny units. Pastel card backgrounds.

Segment page

Waterfall chart + 3–4 bullet insights. Chart chiếm 60%, text 40%.

Financial page

Tables + horizontal bars. No decoration. Numbers aligned right. Tabular nums.

Summary page

Three key takeaways. One outlook. One callout box (teal left accent).

Slide Color Palette

Primary teal#00A7A0Headers, highlights, positive bars
Soft teal#DDF4F2KPI card backgrounds
Soft blue#DCEAF8Secondary segments
Soft purple#ECE7FAAlternate KPI / distribution
White space ratio: 40–50% canvas trống trên mỗi slide. Không fill hết viewport. Combinations: White canvas + teal accent + 1 pastel block + 1 chart. Tối đa 3 màu functional per slide.
{# ===== 08. UI EXAMPLES ===== #}
08

UI Examples

Pattern reference — giải thích tại sao mỗi layout hoạt động trong ngữ cảnh investor / annual report.

KPI cards

Pastel fill + left accent cho phép quét nhanh 5–6 metrics trên 1 row. Số lớn = instant comprehension. Dùng cho stats pages, dashboard hero.

Waterfall charts

Cho thấy contribution từng segment đến tổng. Phù hợp revenue bridge, EBITDA walk. Institutional standard — không cần giải thích legend phức tạp.

Hero blocks

1 headline + 1 hero number + 2 supporting KPIs. Whitespace rộng. Cover slide và landing section cho annual report pages.

Investor slides

Agenda → KPI → Segment waterfall → Financial table → Summary. Nhịp điệu cố định, predictable. Board-ready.

Annual report layouts

Scroll chapter với anchor nav. Mỗi chapter = 1 segment. Interactive AR 2025 là reference implementation.

Distribution charts

Donut hoặc horizontal bar cho portfolio mix (energy / water / urban). Muted palette, teal highlight cho largest segment.

{# ===== 09. ANTI-PATTERNS ===== #}
09

Anti-patterns

Những gì S-DNA không là. Tránh khi borrow selectively cho future pages.

Crypto dashboard

Neon greens, pulsing numbers, dark bg overload.

AdminLTE

Sidebar + boxed widgets + saturated Bootstrap palette.

Dark neon

Glow effects, cyber aesthetic, low readability.

Glassmorphism

Blur layers, frosted cards — conflicts with data clarity.

Too many colors

Rainbow charts, 8+ accent hues per view.

Huge shadows

Drop shadow 20px+ — visual noise, not depth.

Rounded 30px pills

Startup aesthetic — không institutional.

3D charts

Perspective distortion, unreadable values.

Gradient overload

Multi-stop gradients on cards, headers, charts.

Dribbble effects

Fancy micro-interactions, decorative motion.

PowerPoint templates

Stock gradients everywhere, clip-art icons.

Bootstrap admin

Generic dashboard shells, dense tables without hierarchy.
