{# ===== 01 PHILOSOPHY ===== #}
01

Design Philosophy

Enterprise calm · data-first · minimal decoration · high information density · large whitespace · strong hierarchy. Cards first. Charts second. Animation last.

Enterprise calm

Truth > vanity metrics. Institutional, board-ready — not startup pitch.

Data-first

Numbers and insights drive layout. Decoration stays quiet.

Cards first

KPI cards are the default primitive for dashboards and investor pages.

Whitespace breathes

White space > shadows. Borders > gradients. Teal > rainbow.

{# ===== 09 ANTI-PATTERNS ===== #}
09

Anti-patterns

Think Sembcorp Annual Report × Bloomberg × McKinsey — not AdminLTE or Crypto Dashboard.

AdminLTE

Boxed widgets, saturated Bootstrap palette.

Crypto dashboard

Neon, pulsing numbers, dark overload.

Glassmorphism

Blur layers conflict with data clarity.

3D charts

Perspective distortion, unreadable values.

Dribbble effects

Fancy motion, decorative UI noise.

PowerPoint templates

Gradient overload, clip-art icons.

{# ===== 10 COMPATIBILITY ===== #}
